Hi, I'm Z!

I'm a game designer living in Chicago, IL. I've loved games ever since I scraped together every last penny for Kingdom Hearts 2, and never looked back. Nowadays, I spend my time working in Unreal Engine 5 building atmospheric first-person experiences. My MFA thesis was a narrative game about learning to live with old grief, combining post-processing effects with audio from my childhood home videos to anchor the game in real, lived history. I love work that's personal, deeply weird, and unsettling.

Lately, I've been diving deeper into tech art and shader programming. When I'm not working on my projects, I'm hanging out with my cats, writing about horror games and movies, and building more gunpla than my shelves can handle.
